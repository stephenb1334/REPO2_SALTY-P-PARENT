Comprehensive Brand Color Guidelines

This guide details our primary, secondary, and supporting colors along with usage guidelines and sample CSS for consistent UI components.

Brand Color Scheme Guidelines

Our color palette embodies professionalism, approachability, and warm sophistication—perfect for digital and print applications.

Cyan Cobalt Blue
#2656A0

Represents trust, professionalism, and stability.

Aero
#7DC0DE

A refreshing light blue that adds brightness and accessibility.

#53B6B1

A balanced teal tone for success states and interactive elements.

#A2865B

#D0D7CD

Soft neutral grey, ideal for backgrounds and subtle borders.

Usage Guidelines

  • Hierarchy: Use #2656A0 for primary brand elements. Apply #7DC0DE and #53B6B1 for interactive or supportive components.
  • Accessibility: Maintain a contrast ratio of 4.5:1 or higher. Use darker/lighter variants when text overlays colored backgrounds.
  • Consistency: Apply these colors uniformly across websites, social media, and print. For print, request CMYK or Pantone equivalents.
  • Premium Accents: Use #A2865B sparingly to emphasize premium or special features.
  • Neutral Tones: #D0D7CD works best as a background, border, or inactive state, keeping the focus on primary colors.

This combination creates a professional yet approachable brand identity that stands out across diverse media and user touchpoints.
